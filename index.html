Lightweight Systems

For when you have to be mobile, or have light duty sticks, slider, or other support.

Stand Mounted

Teleprompters can be mounted on a stand, and still look through the mirror. Note that there is some room to pan, tilt and zoom, but your shot will have to be relatively locked off.

Forward Facing

Used off camera to simulate an interview, or 'over the shoulder' for scripted dialog.

Jib Mounted

Lightweight, above the lens teleprompters are built for jibs, but of course can be used on any camera. Lightweight mirrored teleprompters are also available.

Handheld

Small, lightweight screens for handheld or Steadycam type camera support.
